# Welcome to the OSEF Wiki

Welcome to the community knowledge base for the **Open Source Engineering Framework (OSEF)**. 

### Mission
> **A repository should be understood before it is transformed.**

OSEF treats codebases not as raw strings of text, but as a semantically connected **Engineering Knowledge Graph (EKG)**. We provide AI agents with deep, structural understanding of your architecture, ensuring that every transformation is context-aware and architecturally sound.
